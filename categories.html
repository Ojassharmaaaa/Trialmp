Driver's License Vehicle Categories

Category 1: Two-Wheeler (Without Gear) +

Documents Required: Age proof, address proof (such as Aadhar card, passport, or utility bill), and Form 1A (medical certificate for applicants over 50 years).

Eligibility: Minimum age of 16 years, with parental consent if under 18.

Additional Requirements: Application fee, recent passport-sized photograph, and a learner's license for practice before the test.

Test Type: Road sign knowledge and basic vehicle handling test conducted by the Regional Transport Office (RTO).

Category 2: Two-Wheeler (With Gear) +

Documents Required: Age proof, address proof, and medical certificate if required by age or health conditions.

Eligibility: Minimum age of 18 years.

Additional Requirements: Recent passport-sized photograph, learner's license, and application fee.

Test Type: Practical riding test to assess control and road safety knowledge at the RTO.

Category 3: Light Motor Vehicle (LMV - Car) +

Documents Required: Proof of age, address, and Form 1A medical certificate if over 50 years.

Eligibility: Minimum age of 18 years.

Additional Requirements: Passport-sized photograph, learner's license, and RTO application fees.

Test Type: Practical driving test assessing skills like parallel parking, reversing, and road awareness.

Category 4: Light Motor Vehicle (Commercial) +

Documents Required: Proof of age, address, and a fitness certificate. Badge or permit may be required for certain states.

Eligibility: Minimum age of 20 years with a valid LMV license for at least one year.

Additional Requirements: Police verification, application fee, and commercial license application form.

Test Type: Practical driving test assessing driving ability, awareness, and knowledge of commercial regulations.

Category 5: Heavy Motor Vehicle (HMV) +

Documents Required: Address and age proof, Form 1A medical certificate, and existing LMV license.

Eligibility: Minimum age of 20 years, with a valid license for LMV or commercial vehicle for at least one year.

Additional Requirements: Recent passport-sized photograph, police verification, and application fees.

Test Type: Practical driving test, including advanced vehicle handling and highway driving skills.

Category 6: Transport Vehicles (Passenger and Goods) +

Documents Required: Age and address proof, medical certificate, and a fitness certificate.

Eligibility: Minimum age of 20 years and at least one year of LMV experience.

Additional Requirements: Police verification, commercial vehicle application fee, and endorsement for passenger transport.

Test Type: Driving test to assess safety, passenger handling, and highway navigation skills.
