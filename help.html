Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1)What is the minimum age to apply for a driving license in India?
The minimum age to apply for a learner's license in India is 16 years for a two-wheeler without gear and 18 years for a motor vehicle with gear.
2) What documents are required to apply for a driving license?
You will need proof of identity (like Aadhaar card, Voter ID), proof of address (like utility bills, rent agreement), proof of age (like birth certificate, passport), passport-size photographs, and the learner's license for a permanent license application.
3) How can I apply for a driving license online?
You can apply for a driving license online through the Parivahan Sewa website by filling out the required forms, uploading necessary documents, paying the application fee, and booking a test slot.
4) What are the different types of driving licenses available?
The main types of driving licenses include learner's license, permanent driving license, commercial driving license, and international driving permit.
5) What is the process for renewing a driving license?
To renew your driving license, visit the Parivahan Sewa website, fill out the renewal application form, upload required documents, pay the renewal fee, and book an appointment at your nearest RTO if needed.
6) How can I prepare for the driving test?
Study the driver's manual, take practice tests online, understand traffic signs and road rules, and practice driving regularly, focusing on essential maneuvers like parallel parking, lane changing, and turning.
7) What are the common traffic violations and their fines?
Common violations include driving without a license (₹5,000 fine), overspeeding (₹1,000 to ₹2,000 fine), driving under the influence (₹10,000 fine and/or imprisonment), and not wearing a helmet (₹1,000 fine)
8) What should I do if I lose my driving license?
If you lose your driving license, you should file a report at the nearest police station, get a copy of the FIR, and then apply for a duplicate license online through the Parivahan Sewa website.
9) What are some important road safety measures?
Always wear a seatbelt, follow speed limits, avoid using mobile phones while driving, never drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs, use indicators when turning, and maintain a safe distance from other vehicles.
10) How can I check the status of my driving license application?
You can check the status of your driving license application by visiting the Parivahan Sewa website, entering your application number, and following the prompts to view the current status.
